About Me

My name is Martijn Meeldijk, I am 20 years of age. I consider myself a highly motivated IT student at UCLL. From an early age, I have been fascinated by computers. When I was 6 years old, I deconstructed and reconstructed old PCs with my grandfather. In the following years, my interest tended more in the direction of software development, as I was interested in computer programs and the algorithms behind them. The second great passion in my life is creating music. Not only do I play various instruments, but I'm also particularly interested in recording and producing music. In a way, the process of working with audio files and making them sound good is very similar to programming. This explains my interest in both fields.

I'm currently undertaking an exchange at Cardiff Metropolitan University.
